About Me

My name is Jungwon Shin, majoring in Information Science and minoring in Business and Game Design. Originally from Korea, I live in New York. I love spoken words and am interested in product design.

The word "Jungwon" is garden in Korean, and Shin can be translated into: new, God, joy and shoes. Jung can mean many different things: affection, to set, to order. Won is origin, root, circle and to want. Among all these possible interpretations, my parents named me Jungwon Shin with the meaning of "to create and be the basis of my own life."

Here's my favorite quote from Sarah Kay - "This world is made out of sugar. It can crumble so easily but don't be afraid to stick your tongue out and taste it."
